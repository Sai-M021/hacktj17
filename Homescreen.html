Creating a Community

At CodeCreatR we aim to create a community of child programmers in order for everyone to benefit, children could post on a safe blog, receiving solutions to problems fast and easily. Our main goal is inspiring children to step up and learn coding so not only as a way to prepare for the future, but also a way to fuel a passion that will inspire children.

Fixing Mistakes

Our Code-0-Matic fixes any error that children may run into when coding, we scour through google and find a result that provides an intuitive and understandable fix and diagnosis of the error you have received, simply click search and we will take care of the rest so you can spend time on what matters.

Inspiring Kids
